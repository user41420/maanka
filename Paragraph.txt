--- SHEEKO 1: WEBIGA IYO ILBAXNIMADA ---
EN: You should look for a river if you get lost in the woods and watch which way it flows, then follow it. You see, humans have always needed water for drinking and farming. So, they would typically build towns along rivers, meaning following one might lead you back to civilization. But some rivers run through empty forests for miles, meaning you could be walking for a while.
SO: Waa inaad raadisaa webi haddii aad ku lunto kaynta, kadibna eegtaa jihada uu u socdo oo aad raacdaa. Dadku mar walba waxay u baahnaayeen biyo ay cabbaan iyo kuwo ay beeraha ku waraabiyaan. Sidaas darteed waxay badanaa magaalooyin ka dhisan jireen hareeraha webiyada, taasoo ka dhigan in raacista webi ay kuu horseedi karto meel dad deggan yihiin. Laakiin qaar ka mid ah webiyada waxay maraan kaymo madhan oo dheer, taasoo ka dhigan inaad socon karto muddo dheer.

--- SHEEKO 2: DHAGAXA CAMBARTA AH ---
EN: She picked up a strange rock from the stream and used it as a doorstop. It sat there for decades and even when thieves broke in and stole her jewelry, they walked right past it. After she died, her relative found the rock and showed it to experts. They confirmed it was amber formed from tree sap millions of years ago. It weighed over 7 pounds, making it one of the biggest ever found. And when the government bought it, they made it a national treasure worth over a million dollars.
SO: Waxay durdurka ka soo qaadatay dhagax yaab leh waxayna u isticmaashay inuu albaabka ceshado. Halkaas ayuu yaalay tobannaan sano, xitaa markii tuugo guriga u soo dhacday oo ay dahabkeedii xadeen way ka gudbeen iyagoo aan qaadan. Markii ay dhimatay kadib ayuu qof qaraabadeeda ah helay dhagaxii wuxuuna tusay khubaro. Waxay xaqiijiyeen inuu ahaa cambar ka samaysmay dheecaan geed malaayiin sano kahor. Wuxuu miisaankiisu ka badnaa todobo rodol, taasoo ka dhigtay mid ka mind ah kuwii ugu waaweynaa ee abid la helo. Markii dowladdu iibsatayna waxay ka dhigtay hanti qaran oo qiimaheedu ka badan yahay hal milyan oo dollar.

--- SHEEKO 3: CIYAARTA SHATARANJIGA ---
EN: He captured the queen, but secretly kept it in his hand. And his opponent was so focused on the board that he didn’t even notice. They continued playing the championship game, and eventually the other player’s pawn reached the opposite side of the board. Which meant he could upgrade it to a queen. But, according to the tournament’s rules, he needed the actual queen to replace it. And since he couldn’t find it, he could only promote it with the rook, giving his enemy an unfair advantage.
SO: Wuxuu qabsaday boqoraddii, laakiin si qarsoodi ah ayuu gacanta ugu haystay. Qofkii uu la ciyaarayayna si aad ah ayuu looxa ugu mashquulsanaa mana uusan dareemin. Waxay sii wadeen ciyaartii horyaalka, ugu dambayna askarigii ciyaaryahanka kale wuxuu gaaray dhinicii kale ee looxa. Taasna waxay ka dhignayd inuu u beddeli karo boqorad. Laakiin sida shuruucda tartanku sheegayaan, wuxuu u baahnaa boqoraddii dhabta ahayd si uu ugu beddelo. Maadaama uusan helin, wuxuu kaliya ugu beddeli karay qalcad, taasoo cadowgiisa siisay faa’iido aan caddaalad ahayn.

--- SHEEKO 4: SAWIRKA IYO MASKAXDA ---
EN: After you choose a picture, the researcher flips it down and pushes forward a different photo hidden behind it. While sliding the one that you actually chose into his lap. And when he shows you the new one, you won’t notice the switch and you might even make up reasons for why you like it. You see, when the photo doesn’t match what you remember, your brain adjusts your memory to make things more consistent. Which makes you believe you picked the other photo all along.
SO: Kadib markaad sawir doorato, cilmi baarihii wuxuu hoos u rogayaa sawirka wuxuuna soo riixayaa sawir kale oo gadaal ku qarsoonaa. Isagoo sawirkii aad dhab ahaan dooratay si tartiib ah u dhigaya dhabtiisa. Markuu ku tuso sawirka cusubna ma dareemi doontid beddelka, xitaa waxaad samayn kartaa sababo aad u sheegto inaad u jeceshahay. Marka sawirku uusan la mid noqon waxa aad xasuusato, maskaxdaadu waxay beddeshaa xasuusta si ay wax walba isugu ekaadaan. Taasna waxay kaa dhigaysaa inaad rumaysato inaad sawirka kale dooratay bilowgiiba.

--- SHEEKO 5: FARSHAXANKA INDHAHA ---
EN: He started painting walls as a teen and his art spread across the city. People recognized it everywhere, making him a legend. But he got sick with a disease that paralyzed his body, and for 7 years, he could only move his eyes. So, a team of programmers built him a pair of glasses with a camera. It tracked his eyes and moved a cursor on the screen. He would stare at a color to select it before moving the cursor to draw lines as the device turned them into brush strokes. The team projected the art onto buildings across the world.
SO: Wuxuu bilaabay rinjiyeynta darbiyada isagoo dhalinyaro ah, farshaxankiisuna wuxuu ku faafay magaalada oo dhan. Dadku meel walba way ka garanayeen, taasoo ka dhigtay halyeey caan ah. Laakiin waxaa ku dhacay cudur jirkiisa curyaamiyay, muddo todobo sano ahna wuxuu kaliya dhaqaajin karay indhihiisa. Sidaas darteed koox barnaamij sameeyayaal ah ayaa u sameeyay muraayado leh kamarad. Waxay la soconaysay indhihiisa waxayna dhaqaajinaysay fallaarta shaashadda. Wuxuu eeggi jiray midab si uu u doorto kadibna wuxuu dhaqaajin jiray fallaarta si uu xariijimo u sawiro, qalabkuna wuxuu u beddeli jiray raadad burush. Kooxdu waxay farshaxankiisa ku soo bandhigeen darbiyo dhismayaal ku yaal dunida oo dhan.

--- SHEEKO 6: DUULIYAHA IYO GANTAALKA ---
EN: The pilot would pull the ejection handle, igniting a small rocket under his seat. This would cause his body to be slammed with over 1,000 lbs of force, all in less than a second. And this would squish his spine downwards, packing it so tightly that in some cases, his spine wouldn’t be able to stretch back. Which could leave him a little shorter than he was before.
SO: Duuliyuhu wuxuu jiidi jiray gacanta kursiga laga tuuro, taasoo shidaysay gantaal yar oo kursigiisa hoostiisa yaal. Taasi waxay jirkiisa ku rideysay xoog ka badan kun rodol wax ka yar hal ilbiriqsi gudaheed. Waxayna hoos u cadaadinaysay laf dhabartiisa si aad u xooggan, mararka qaarna laf dhabartu dib uma kala bixi karin. Taasina waxay ka dhigi kartay inuu wax yar ka gaabnaado sidii uu markii hore ahaa.

--- SHEEKO 7: KHAMAARKA IYO XADDAADA TALEEFANKA ---
EN: He used her fingerprints to open her phone and peeled back her eyelids, using her face to unlock her bank account. You see, he was deep in debt from gambling. And when his ex-girlfriend got sick, he mixed cold medicine into her food, making her fall into a deep sleep. He sent himself over $20,000, which he used to pay off his debt before running away to gamble even more. But when she woke up, she had several alerts from her bank.
SO: Wuxuu isticmaalay faraha gacanteeda si uu u furo taleefankeeda kadibna kor ayuu u qaaday baalasha indhaheeda si wajigeeda loogu furo akoonkeeda bangiga. Wuxuu galay deyn banyak oo ka dhalatay khamaar. Markii saaxiibtiisii hore xanuunsatay ayuu dawo hargab ugu daray cuntadeeda taasoo ku ridday hurdo aad u dheer. Wuxuu isu diray in ka badan labaatan kun oo doolar, lacagtaasna wuxuu ku bixiyay deyntiisii kahor intuusan baxsan si uu khamaar kale ugu sii ciyaaro. Laakiin markii ay soo toostay waxay heshay digniino badan oo uga yimid bangigeeda.

--- SHEEKO 8: BADBAADADA BARAFKA (AVALANCHE) ---
EN: If you get buried by an avalanche, the snow will compress you on all sides, making it hard to tell which way is up. So, if you try to dig your way out, you could end up going the wrong way and suffocate to death. Instead, some people say to cover your face and make an air pocket, then start drooling. Whichever way the drool falls is down, so you should dig the opposite way.
SO: Haddii baraf soo rogmaday ku aaso, barafku dhinac walba ayuu kaa cadaadinayaa taasoo adkeynaysa inaad ogaato dhanka sare. Sidaas darteed haddii aad isku daydo inaad qodato si aad uga baxdo, waxaad u qodi kartaa dhinac qaldan oo neef qabatin ayaad u dhiman kartaa. Halkii aad sidaas sameen laheed, dadka qaar waxay sheegaan inaad wajigaaga daboolato oo meel hawo leh samaysato kadibna candhuuf iska daysid. Dhinaca ay candhuuftu u dhacdo waa hoose, marka waa inaad u qoddaa dhinaca ka soo horjeeda.
